--- layout: post title: Hadoop Development Steadily Rising date: '2007-07-15T11:03:00.000+01:00' author: Tom White tags: - Hadoop modified_time: '2007-07-15T11:30:24.196+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8898949683610477251.post-170973993722453844 blogger_orig_url: http://www.tom-e-white.com/2007/07/hadoop-development-steadily-rising.html --- Judging by this graph showing posts to the dev list (on Gmane) the rate is currently at about 50 posts a day. This has roughly doubled since the beginning of the year. Some of the increase is down to the momentum behind Hbase (which provides Bigtable-like capabilities on top of Hadoop), but I think it is also down to general growth - more people seem to be participating in development than ever before. This is great! Obviously the risk is that at such a rate of development Hadoop becomes unstable, but so far it looks like it's under control - the (informal) feedback I've had tells me the 0.13.0 release is one of the most stable we've done.
